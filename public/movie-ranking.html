The Best Movies According to Michael

My top 3 movies of all time.

Remember the Titans

This is, by far, my favorite movie of all time. It tells a true story of a newly formed high school football team that brings an entire community together, despite all of the racial tensions of the day.

Lord of the Rings Trilogy

This is my favorite Christian, allegorical movie series.

The Patriot

This is a very inspiring telling of the American Revolution.
